Things I didn’t do in 2015 and things I won’t probably do in 2016

in Uncategorized

on 16 January 2016

While all the people around me are making list of their achievements I decided to ponder over some things I didn’t do in 2015. I wouldn’t call them underachievements, far from that, they just didn’t happen:

  1. I didn’t learn how to plan my trips although I worked really hard on that (or rather: I tried to think I was going to work on that). I came to terms with the fact that itineraries are not my friends, fortunately, my fellow travellers seemed to understand it.
  2. I didn’t travel to Madagascar, although I started 2015 with the thought it was going to be my next big destination.
  3. I didn’t spend a weekend in Spain to get the taste of the country. It was much better than that! I spent there the whole ten days! Booked the plane ticket, without even considering the costs and I simply went there – a year without Spanish is not a good year.
  4. I didn’t study Spanish too much. Well, to be honest, I didn’t study it at all. I still read crime stories in Spanish but it seems that vocabulary of murders, autopsies and horrific crimes is not really useful in everyday conversation. Or might simply mix with the wrong crowd?
  5. I didn’t come back to learning any language I used to learn before. I hid the coursebood and grammar books somewhere on the back shelves to muffle my remorse.
  6. I didn’t learn how to edit travel videos although I started to shoot them while travelling
  7. I wasn’t a regular blogger – it’s really difficult, too many things are happening in the real world to spend too much time online.
  8. I didn’t try Snap or Periscope or Twitter – each minute more in the social media equals each minute less on books and films.
  9. I didn’t travel alone in 2015 and I don’t mind it.
  10. I didn’t go through all the photos I took last year – I’m tired of too many photos.

 

I didn’t do all those things although I had planned to do them and now I’m starting to wonder… What won’t I manage to do do next year?  Well, let’s think…

  1. I won’t manage with any projects of “one photo a day’, ‘one picture a day’ or ’10 foreign words a day’. Half a month has passed and I hasn’t started anything yet, too late now.
  2. I won’t learn how to plan trips. It was supposed to be different this year – I’ve got a list of books, film and blogs to read/watch before my trip to Iran, but there’re 10 days left and the chances are getting lower each day.
  3. I won’t go to Africa – for different reasons.
  4. I won’t go to Latin America – I’m really sad about it.
  5. I won’t go where I have planned to go. I don’t know why yet, but for the last five years I’ve never gone to the places I planned to go first so it’s likely to happen this time as well.
  6. I won’t study any language too much. I love using the languages, I hate studying them. It’s kind of hard to achieve anything with this kind of attitude.
  7. I won’t learn to edit films. Or maybe I will?
  8. I will be a failure at regular blogging as well – I’ve started so many topics but there is so little time to write the endings.
  9. I won’t read all the book and I won’t watch all the film I would love to – so little time.
  10. I won’t worry about the things I won’t do.

 

No matter how many thing I won’t do in 2016, I’m still positve it is going to be a very good year – just like 2015 when I also failed at many things.

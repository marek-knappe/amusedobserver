Are packing cubes really worth the money?

in Uncategorized

on 14 March 2016

Nonsense, unnecessary extra weight, uncomofortable, take too much space, too expensive – this is what I heard when I asked people about packing cubes. Interestingly enough, all those negative opinions were voiced by people who had never used packing cubes! The owners told different stories – they shared they love for this kind of organizers!
I decided to check out myself…

Packing cubes in your suitcase

They are absolutely perfect! Everything is finally ordered and stays in place! When you open your suitcase nothing falls out or moves. Packing everything back in when you are about to finish your holiday is easier than ever before.

Packing cubes in your backpack

I expected they wouldn’t be as much useful in a backpack as they are in a suitcase. Oh, how wrong I was!
When you open your suitcase you can see almost everything immediatelly, and if you need to find something fast you will find it fast – you might turn everything upsided down at times but generally, access to your stuff is pretty simple. It is not so easy with a backpack, though as the clothes and accessories are put in layers and even you you have a zip in front it might be difficult to get something out. It all suddenly has become much simpler with packing cubes! I simply take out my two (or three) packing cubes, look for something I need and then simply put them back again without causing any mess :

Sizes

Before you buy your packing cubes check carefully what sizes are offered – they might differ between different brands and one will offer size S for something that is size M in a different company. I bought two packing cubes of Sea to Summit in size S (25 x 19 x 9.8 cm, weight 60g) and one in M size (35 x 25 x 9 cm, weight 86g). Size M I find usefull in a suitcase – for long dresses and shirts that shouldn’t be rolled (that’s how I pack my T-shirts to make more room). Size S is perfect for my backpack – two packing cubes ware enough to pack all my clothes and towels.

Why are they comfortable?

  • everything is in order,
  • you can see what is inside and where through the mesh top,
  • colours make it easier to know what is where (especially if you share you suitcase with someone),
  • long zips make packing easy,
  • this way you can put more stuff than when you pack everything into plastic bags,
  • there are many sizes – you can choose the size that is the most convinient for you.

Packind cubes are much better than plastic bags (which rustle and don’t keep the shape) or compression bags (which are not so comfortable for un/packing). Below you can see my packing cubes size M and S – they are not fully packed – I’ve become so good at packing that more often I have too much room in my suitcase than too little room!

A year with packing cubes

Packing cubes turned out to be the most useful travel accesories I bought last year! They are superb both in my suitcase and backpack and I also use them during weekend trips where I go away only with a small city backpack (then everything goes into one packing cube).

Since I bought my packing cubes there hasn’t been a trip without them!
